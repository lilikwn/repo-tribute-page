Here's timeline of Ed Sheeran life :


  • 1991 - Ed Sheeran was born in Halifax, England

  • 2005 - Sheeran began recording in 2005, which lead to the release of his first EP, The Orange Room EP. He was just 13 at the time

  • 2006 - Ed Sheeran released his first album in 2006 which was self titled

  • 2007 - Ed released the album 'Want Some' in 2007

  • 2008 - in 2008, when ed was 16 he moved from Halifax to London to pursue his music career

  • 2009 - In 2009 Ed Sheeran played an extremly impressive 312 gigs!!!. he was inspired to play so many because of James Morrison. James played a large 200 in one year and Ed was keen to beat that record. He beat this record with ease.

  • 2010 - In February 2010 Ed posted a video on YouTube that got hugely noticed. It was the original recording of his song 'You need me, I don't need you' It got so well known the rapper Example asked Ed to tour wiht him.

  • Jan 8, 2011 - On 8 January 2011, Sheeran released his final independent EP, No. 5 Collaborations Project featuring grime artists including Wiley, JME, Devlin, Sway and Ghetts

  • Jan 24, 2011 - The A Team entered the UK Singles Chart at number three, selling over 58,000 copies in the first week

  • Aug 28, 2011 - You Need Me, I Don't Need You was released on 28 August as the second single from the album. The second single peaked at number four on the UK Singles Chart

  • Sep 12, 2011 - Ed Sheeran's debut album + was released on the 12th Sept 2011 and sold an impressive 102,000 copies in the first week.

  • Mar 1, 2012 - Ed Sheeran annouced that his new album was underway

  • Aug 22, 2012 - in 2012 Ed headlined a stage at his biggest festival yet, V fest. he had played a few ungerground festivals prior like the Red Fest but nothing to this scale
